About E-Bundle

Empowering Ethiopian students with next-generation learning tools since 2023

Our Mission

Making Quality Education Accessible to Every Ethiopian Student

E-Bundle was founded by a team of Ethiopian educators and technologists who saw the need for personalized, affordable learning solutions. We combine the power of artificial intelligence with the Ethiopian national curriculum to create an engaging learning experience.

Today, we serve over 50,000 students across all regions of Ethiopia, helping them prepare for national exams and build a strong foundation for their future careers.
